# 📊 HR Attendance & Workforce Productivity Dashboard

### Power BI | DAX | Power Query | SQL | Excel

> **Executive HR Analytics Case Study**
>
> Built a 2-page executive Power BI dashboard to analyze attendance patterns of **99 employees** over **3 months**, uncovering workforce risks, absenteeism trends, and policy violations to support data-driven HR decisions.

---

# 📌 Executive Summary

Traditional attendance reports show **what happened**.

This dashboard explains **why it happened**, **who is at risk**, and **what actions HR leaders should take next**.

The solution combines Power BI, DAX, and Power Query to transform raw attendance records into an executive reporting system with actionable insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

AtliQ Technologies needed a reporting solution that could answer critical workforce questions such as:

- Is Work From Home usage within company policy?
- Why is absenteeism cost increasing?
- Which employees are at risk of disengagement?
- Are there employees with zero office presence?
- Is sick leave driving absenteeism?

# 📈 Key Insights

### 📌 1. Leave Without Pay Increased by 72%

LWP increased from **21.5 days (April)** to **37 days (June)**, indicating rising productivity loss and workforce disengagement.

---

### 📌 2. WFH Policy Breach

Company-wide WFH adoption increased by **26.7%**, exceeding the defined policy threshold.

---

### 📌 3. Flight Risk Detection

Multiple employees displayed attendance behaviors associated with potential disengagement, including prolonged WFH dependency and increasing Leave Without Pay.

---

### 📌 4. Sick Leave Spike

Sick Leave increased by over **300%** in May, suggesting possible workplace health or environmental concerns.

---

### 📌 5. Zero Office Presence

Several employees recorded **zero office attendance** during the entire reporting period, highlighting potential policy or data governance issues.

---

### 📌 6. Friday WFH Pattern

Friday recorded the highest Work From Home rate, indicating a consistent weekly attendance pattern.

---

### 📌 7. Workforce Segmentation

Attendance analysis revealed two distinct employee groups:

- High LWP + Low Sick Leave
- High LWP + High Sick Leave

Both groups require different HR interventions.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

Based on dashboard findings, three strategic actions are recommended:

### ✅ Burnout Intervention

Identify high-risk employees and conduct manager check-ins before productivity declines further.

### ✅ WFH Governance

Strengthen Work From Home approval policies and monitor Friday attendance trends.

### ✅ Data Governance

Audit attendance records for employees with zero office presence and improve attendance tracking processes.
